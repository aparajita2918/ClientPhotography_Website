About Veena


Praveena Venugopal is a photographer born in India, currently based in Chennai. Growing up around a father who photographed every little moments of her life, she held her first camera as a child. Little did she know she would grow up to become a photographer passionate about photographing people. She engages with and enjoys every genre of photography and have exhibited photographs from her travel and portraiture collection. Being fascinated about psychology, human connection, body language helps her excel in portrait photography whether it is fashion or documentary.
She is also passionate about social documentary photography and building relationships through them. She has worked along with Scottish Trans Alliance and LGBT Youth Scotland to produce 'Beyond Gender' a documentary photography project that brings forward the stories of the Transgender community in Scotland.
This was well received and went on to be exhibited at Poetry Club (BBC Scotland) and published on Gaylaxy Magazine.
She has also worked with African Community in Scotland, Blue Cross of India, American India Foundation, and other organisations that work with sensitive communities. She has also travelled around India, Nepal, Europe, UK, and USA photographing diverse people, places and culture.
